Project Overview

ChargedUP: Behind The Scenes of an RC Car is an interactive educational website that connects battery chemistry (Chemistry 30S) with motor physics and kinematics (Physics 30S) through the real-world application of powering an RC toy car.

Learning Goals

  1. Battery Chemistry: Understand how AA batteries (Alkaline and NiMH) use redox reactions to produce electrons and store electrical energy.
  2. Motor Physics: Model how electric current creates magnetic fields using the right-hand rule and how motors convert electrical energy to rotation.
  3. Kinematics & Forces: Apply Newton's Laws and kinematics equations to calculate how motor torque creates motion in an RC car.
  4. Real-World Integration: Connect all concepts to the practical application of building and understanding RC car performance.

Target Audience

Grade 11 students in Manitoba taking Chemistry 30S and Physics 30S, aligned with Manitoba Education curriculum outcomes.
